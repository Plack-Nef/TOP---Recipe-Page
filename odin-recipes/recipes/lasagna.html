Lasagna


Heaven can be a place on earth

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Onions and Garlic
  • A Can of Crushed Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Fresh Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning,
    Fennel Seeds and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, Mozzarella and Ricotta
  • An Egg

Steps

  • Make the meat sauce.
  • Cook the noodles.
  • Make the Cheese Mixture
  • Layer the Lasagna according to the Recipe Instructions
  • Cover with Foil and bake
  • Let the Lasagna Rest before Serving
