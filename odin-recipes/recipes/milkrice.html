German Rice Pudding


Soft and Creamy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white rice, long or medium grain
  • 1/4 cup sugaar
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1 (2-inch long piece) vanilla bean,
    split open; or 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ground cinnamon, to taste
  • Sugar

Steps

  • Gather the Ingredients
  • Mix the rice, sugar and salt in a large saucepan
  • Stir in the milk and add the piece of vanilla bean or the vanilla extract
  • Place the mixture over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring often
  • Reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes, or until the rice
    is soft and the mixture becomes thick. Stir often.
  • Serve
